## Upload your files

![upload files](https://gyazo.com/443ba15389754b2f43dd3fc35be711c4.png)

- [github.com/progfay/code-refactor-20190225](https://github.com/progfay/code-refactor-20190225/) の`Upload files`をClick

![Propose changes](https://gyazo.com/da4828e318a767d396d37106ec3aadc6.png)

1. Drag & Dropでファイルを追加する (フォルダごとも可能)
2. `Create a new branch`を選択
3. `Propose changes`をClick
